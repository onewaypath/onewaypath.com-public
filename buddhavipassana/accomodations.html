About

The Buddhavipassana Meditation Centre is operated by One Way Path Group in mid-town, Toronto, Canada. We provide insight meditation based on the four foundations of mindfulness in the Therevada Buddhist Tradition.

    • Retreat Accomodations

    Retreats are held at the Buddhavipassana Meditation Centre near Christie Street and St. Clair Avenue West in Toronto. Rooms are normally shared two people of the same stated gender to a room and each person has a separate bed for sleeping. The centre also has a meditation room, an eating area and a kitchenette for snacks.
