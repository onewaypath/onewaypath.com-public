About

Insight Meditation Courses

Spiritual Leaders

The Buddhavipassana Meditation Centre offers extensive, in depth and authentic training in the Buddhist method of spiritual growth and development known as insight meditation. These courses also serve as an introduction to Buddhism as practiced in the northern Thai tradition. Courses are by donation and we request a minimum 15-day commitment from meditators who are new to this style of practice.

Leaders

Ajahn Tong
Sirimangalo

The late Most Venerable Chao Khun Phra Prommongkol Vi (Most Ven. Ajarn Tong Sirimangalo) spent over 6 decades teaching Satipatthana Vipassana Meditation and establishing and expanding Meditation Centres throughout Thailand and the rest of the world. He ordained as a novice monk in 1934 and as a full fledged monk in 1944 giving him 75 years as a monk and 85 years in robes before his death in 2019. Ajahn Tong and his teachings remain a tramendous source of wisdom and inspiration for current teachers of the Vipassana Meditation method he popularized.

Ajahn Thanat
Chindaporn

The late Ajahn Thanat Chindaporn, a senior disciple of Most Venerable Chao Khun Phra Prommongkol Vi (Most Venerable Ajarn Tong Sirimangalo), was a senior teacher of Satipatthana Vipassana Meditation in the Therevada Buddhist tradition of Thailand and was the director of the International Meditation Department at Wat Phra Dhatu Sri Chomtong Voravihara, Chomtong, Chiangmai, which was established in the year 2000. Ajahn Thanat passed away in 2022.

Ajahn Kathryn
Chindaporn

Kathryn Chindaporn, is a senior disciple of Most Venerable Chao Khun Phra Prommongkol Vi (Most Venerable Ajarn Tong Sirimangalo), having studied Satipatthana Vipassana Meditation since 1986. Ajarn Kathryn has been teaching Vipassana for over 30 years, teaching in Thailand and her birthplace, Washington State, and conducting courses in North America, and Europe since 1988. Ajarn Kathryn is currently the director of the International Department of our main Temple in Chomtong, Thailand
