About

One Way Path Communities builds multi-residential housing in liveable communities in and around the Greater Toronto Hamilton Area. These projects present an opportunity for equity investors to earn high rates of return with limited risk. Intrested equity and debt partners are invited to contact info@onewaypath.com for more information.

ALEX YOUNG

Finance Partner

First Nations Business Infrastructure

Working with local first nations and a team of legal and financial advisors, Alex successfully assigned project risks and benefits between project developers and affected communities. The risk allocation deal resulted in private sector construction of the $2 billion Equinox Gold mining project in Geraldton near Thunder Bay Ontario.

Ontario Health Infrastructure

Alex was a direct advisor to the Minister of Health and Long-Term Care during the period when the Ontario Government built or expanded a record 27 hospitals on-time and on-budget by shifting risk to the insurance industry. He later studied construction risk allocation through a Global Professional Master's of Law at the University of Toronto.

International Risk Allocation

Working for Global Affairs Canada, Alex successfully re-opened high-risk Canadian businesses in Vietnam by negotiating a risk allocation agreement among public authorities, businesses and non-profits. He later represented Canada at the United Nations General Assembly in New York to achieve support for a key Canadian initiative by a resounding 3 to 1 margin.
