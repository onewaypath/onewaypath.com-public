    • Renovation Plans: Phase I

    The first phase of the renovation project will include some needed structural repairs and renovation of the old shop on the ground floor. When these renovations are complete, the ground floor can be used for as a small meditation centre. Phase I includes the following:

    1. Re-enforce ground, first and second floors on the north side;
    2. Repair weather damage to exterior facade;
    3. Extend heating to the old shop on the ground floor;
    4. Upgrade ground floor electrical system;
    5. Install a toilet in the old shop;
    6. Add a wall in the old shop to create meditation reporting room; and
    7. Dry wall and painting upgrades in the old shop.
