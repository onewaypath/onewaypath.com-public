About

The On-Nūm Meditation Center is operated by One Way Path in Lieberose, Germany. We provide insight meditation based on the four foundations of mindfulness in the Therevada Buddhist Tradition.

    This retreat is intended for beginners to take part. For many people, this will be their first exposure to meditation practice. Your teacher will adjust the retreat according to your level of comfort. If you have any concerns during the retreat, each student meets with the teacher daily, when they may ask all questions and voice concerns.

    During the retreat, talking is mostly not permitted with very limited exceptions. Some exceptions are when organizing meals or for other logistical reasons and when discussing your practice with the meditation teacher.

    Sleeping accommodations for the first retreat will be by reservation at a local guest house which will charge a fee for overnight accommodation and food. This fee does not include any remuneration for the teaching or the teachers’ travel expenses. We therefore invite you to make a contribution to those costs after the retreat according your means and the value, in your opinion, that you derive from the course. Since we are unable to put a price on the value of the Buddha’s teaching, as a rule, we will not give you advice or any suggestion on how much to contribute. If you choose to make a contribution, the amount is entirely up to you.
