One Way Path Group

Alex Young, MPA, GPLLM

President & Chief Executive Officer

Alex Young is Co-Founder, President and Chief Executive Officer of One Way Path Group. In this role he co-ordinates all projects sponsored by the company with ultimate responsibility for financial, design and operational matters.

Alex draws on a background of public policy, law and finance, to solve public policy problems and generate higher than average profits for investors without additional risk.

Expertise


Alex comes to the real estate development industry from a 20-year career in public and private sector finance most recently in relation to large infratructure projects at a leading First Nations law firm including in the mining, transportation and energy sectors.

Alex continues to practice law and acts as general conunsel for One Way Path Group. His practice includes corporate law, wills and estates and tax planning particularly to the extent that matters in those areas intersect with the work of the company.

Prior to private sector practice, Alex practiced social and financial community design at various orders of government including directly advising the Deputy Premier of Ontario on community health policy and negotiating for Canada at the United Nations General Assembly in New York.

Alex is Co-Founder and a pro-bono teacher in residence at the Buddhavipassana Meditation Centre in Toronto. His work in developing communities centred on insight meditation was recognized in 2014 by the Governor of Chiangmai Province and by Thailand's federal Ministry of Culture, who appointed him a Benefactor of Buddhism in 2020.
