About

Activities funded by the foundation include insight meditation in the Therevada Buddhist Tradition and the protection and preservation of endangered languages and cultures.

    Overview

    The Buddhavipassana Meditation Centre is located in mid-town, Toronto, Canada. The centre provides insight meditation training based on the four foundations of mindfulness in the Therevada Buddhist Tradition.

    Cost Recovery

    The costs for accomodation and food are recovered in two ways:

    1. By donation for new students who book for a 15 day basic course or returning students who book for a 10 day retreat
    2. General donations receiced other than after a retreat

    Atisundara Foundation Funding

    Due to COVID-19, revenue for 2020 and 2021 was lower than usual and is not a reliable measure of expected annual revenue. Prior to 2020, annual renveue averaged approximatedly $50,000. Therefore, an annual subsidy of approximatedly $44,000 from the Atisundara Foundation is required to maintain operation of the centre.
