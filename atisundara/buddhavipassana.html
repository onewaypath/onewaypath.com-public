    Overview

    The Buddhavipassana Meditation Centre is located in mid-town, Toronto, Canada. The centre provides insight meditation training based on the four foundations of mindfulness in the Therevada Buddhist Tradition.

    Cost Recovery

    The costs for accommodation and food are recovered in two ways:

    1. By donation for new students who book for a 15 day basic course or returning students who book for a 10 day retreat
    2. General donations received other than after a retreat

    Atisundara Foundation Funding

    Due to COVID-19, revenue for 2020 and 2021 was lower than usual and is not a reliable measure of expected annual revenue. Prior to 2020, annual revenue averaged approximately $50,000. Therefore, an annual subsidy of approximately $36,000 from the Atisundara Foundation is required to maintain operation of the centre.

    Renovation Plans

    Renovation plans for the Buddhavipassana Meditation Centre will add a net total of five bedrooms along with a large meditation hall and an outdoor gazebo. The total maximum capacity after the renovation will be 8 bedrooms and three bathrooms.

    A small "L" shaped gazebo will be added to the backyard at the north end of the property. And two parking spaces will be added at on the south side.

    In the basement, four bedrooms will be added along with a kitchenette, and a bathroom.

    On the second floor, the meditation room on the south end will be converted into a bedroom and a sitting area will be added next to the stairs.

    The attic will be finished to contain a bedroom, bathroom and meditation hall.
